Privacy Policy for Pieces Sport

Illuminati Linkage Company Limited respects your privacy and has developed this privacy policy (the “Privacy Policy”) to explain how we collect, use, and share information from or about you in connection with your use of the Pieces Sport which features a variety of soccer-themed mini-games where players can earn score points and compete on the In-Game Leaderboard.

This Privacy Statement explains our practices, including your choices, as well as the disclosure of certain information, including your personal information, by the Illuminati.

What sets Pieces Sport apart is its unique Prediction Challenge, where players can put their football knowledge to the test by predicting the outcomes of real-world football matches. By correctly guessing the scores in response to our carefully curated questions, players have the chance to earn valuable rewards, adding a competitive edge to the experience.

As they participate in games and challenges, players accumulate points, which can be claimed through Pieces Sport’s comprehensive Leaderboard. This system provides an exciting opportunity for players to win various prizes, enhancing their overall experience on Pieces Sport.
